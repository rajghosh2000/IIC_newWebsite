Major Focus

Start-up supporting Mechanism in HEIs. Establish Function Ecosystem for Scouting Ideas and Pre-incubation of Ideas. Develop better Cognitive Ability for Technology Students.

Function

To conduct various innovation and entrepreneurship-related activities prescribed by Central MIC in time bound fashion.Organize idea competition, mini-challenges etc. with the involvement of industries.Identify and reward innovations and share success stories and mainy more.

Hackathons

Organizing Hackathons on building new innovations and reward the best. The participants work in small groups in a unique environment that encourages creative thinking and leads to surprisingly innovative new concepts, ideas, and prototypes.

Intellectual Talks

Organizing Seminers by some of the best Entrepreneurs of the country and encouraging new ideas to come up.The aim of entrepreneurial seminar is to prepare future economy engineers to potentially their own entrepreneurial path.

About Institutes Innovation Council Tezpur Univetsity

The cell was introduced under the Vigilence of MHRD's Innitiative. This waas according to the EDC plans. The Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) was started with the object of promoting specialized knowledge in the field of entrepreneurship development. In view of the worldwide shortage of jobs (both government and private sectors) leading to unemployment problems and lack of proper utilization of human resources; the cell strives to identify talented youth to entrepreneurial works. The cell plans to organise various programmes regarding entrepreneurship development.
